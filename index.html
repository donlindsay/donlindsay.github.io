AI Stack — local-first KG+LLM workbench
github.com/donlindsay

A private, offline-first AI workbench running natively in WSL2: AllegroGraph (RDF + SPARQL + RDFS++ reasoning) as the symbolic brain, and a free engine×model matrix — Ollama and llama.cpp serving Nemotron & Twin-Llama on a 12 GB Blackwell GPU — as the neural shell, behind a FastAPI shim and a native Windows Avalonia app skinned as a guitar pedalboard. Default: llama.cpp × Nemotron (~180 tok/s, CUDA sm_120). vLLM is shelved (dormant, swap-in-able).
