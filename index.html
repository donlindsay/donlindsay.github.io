IRON & FEATHERS

AI Stack — local-first KG+LLM workbench
github.com/donlindsay

A private, offline-first AI workbench running natively in WSL2: AllegroGraph (RDF + SPARQL + RDFS++ reasoning) as the symbolic brain, and a single local runtime — llama.cpp serving Nemotron & Twin-Llama for chat and embeddings on a 12 GB Blackwell GPU — as the neural shell, behind a FastAPI shim and a native Windows Avalonia app skinned as a guitar pedalboard. Default: llama.cpp × Nemotron (~180 tok/s, CUDA sm_120). Ollama, vLLM, n8n & ComfyUI are optional, swap-in-able add-ins. The new SHELL pedal is an agentic chat-editor that runs shell commands under a permission policy, with provenance written to AllegroGraph.
