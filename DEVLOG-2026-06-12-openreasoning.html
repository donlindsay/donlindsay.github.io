DEVLOG — 2026-06-12 — OpenReasoning-Nemotron-7B Adoption and Graph-Based Enhancements

Model Adoption: OpenReasoning-Nemotron-7B was adopted as the default local model at Q8_0, replacing nemotron-4B, following evaluation showing superior operator competency (9/10 vs. 7/10) and NemoClaw recall (9–10/10), with Q8_0 chosen for maximum fidelity headroom over Q5_K_M.

Critical Bug Fixes: Three AG-fluency battery bugs were resolved: (1) silent credential drop in HTTP queries (401 errors), (2) illegal FROM <bare-repo-name> prompts, and (3) a stale catalog count. A latent model-swapping bug (localllm._write_llamacpp_target, and its vLLM twin) was fixed to prevent reversion to twin-llama on every sync.

Performance and Fidelity: OpenReasoning-Nemotron-7B outperformed nemotron-4B in operator competency and NemoClaw recall. Perplexity on a 159 KB corpus of the stack's own docs: Q4_K_M 122.3, Q5_K_M 108.3, Q6_K 109.9, Q8_0 113.3 — Q4 measurably least faithful, Q5/Q6/Q8 statistically tied (a fidelity plateau from Q5). RDF read/write floored at 0/6 across every model — a genuine capability limit, the harness verified by self-test.

Reasoning Limitation: OpenReasoning's <think> mode cannot be disabled — it ignores both enable_thinking=false and NVIDIA's "detailed thinking off" phrase (it is Qwen2-architecture, not Llama-Nemotron) — and overruns the token budget, a real cost now paid on every default-path generation.

Documentation Updates: Two new pages published — an explainer, "Facts Come From the Graph, Not the Prompt" (generated content must source its facts from AllegroGraph, not the prompt), under a new Explainers section; and a graph-grounded Fitness Report superseding the older AllegroGraph one.

Next Step: Queued development of a hardened NL→SPARQL skill — schema/prefix injection from the graph plus a parser-validated retry loop — to scaffold around the SPARQL-generation floor (a capability gap, not a fidelity gap).

Drafted by SHELL running on the new OpenReasoning-Nemotron-7B Q8_0 default model.
