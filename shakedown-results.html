Fix: matrix-cell click should keep the Reaper tab active (set engine/model without navigating).

  6. Cross-cutting (cosmetic): UI Engine/Model pedals don't re-sync after a backend /local-engine reset (e.g. matrix-cell engage / API reset) — pedal skin can lag the active backend. Registry triple counts are stale until registry.rebuild().
